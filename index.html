Andrea Rosà


I am Postdoctoral Researcher at the Faculty of Informatics of Università della Svizzera italiana (USI) in Lugano, Switzerland. I'm working in the Dynamic Analysis Group (DAG). My research interests include dynamic program analysis, managed languages and runtimes, dynamic compilers, benchmarking, concurrency and parallelism, query data processing, and big-data analytics.

I received the PhD in Informatics from USI in 2018, advised by Prof. Walter Binder. Previously, I received the MSc and BSc in Computer Science Engineering from Politecnico di Milano.
