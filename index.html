Andrea Rosà


I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Faculty of Informatics of Università della Svizzera italiana (USI) in Lugano, Switzerland. I'm working in the Dynamic Analysis Group (DAG). My research interests include concurrency and parallelism, program analysis, virtual machines and big-data computing.

I received the PhD in Informatics from Università della Svizzera italiana (USI) in 2018, advised by Prof. Walter Binder. Previously, I received the MSc and BSc in Computer Science Engineering from Politecnico di Milano.
